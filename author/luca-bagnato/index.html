Welcome!

I am a PhD Candidate in Economics at the University of Zurich. My research interests lie at the intersection of political economics, economic history and public economics. I use fine-grained archival and contemporary data to study how institutions and policies affect individual values and actions. My current research focuses on how the capacity of the state to tax its citizens affects their values and their actions.
